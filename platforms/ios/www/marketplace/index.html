HOW IT WORKS

AS A BUYER

1. Click "Buy Now" on the item you want to buy

2. Select "Verify and buy in store"

3. We notify you once the item is at our store for you to come verify and close sale, however delivery option is available at a separate agree fee. We verify it and bring it to you.

4. See and test the item and pay either in store or where you are if you opted for delivery.

We handle the entire transaction process to give you safe and secure buying experience!

HOW IT WORKS

AS A SELLER

1. Click "Sell an item" to Post your item (Do not input the price or your personal details such as phone no. and location on the description area)

2. The selling Price = Your Price + Our Commision (You have control on the selling price since we stand for transparency)
